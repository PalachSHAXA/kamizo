================================================================================
                        MEETINGS VOTING SYSTEM (KEY FEATURE)
================================================================================

IMPLEMENTATION DETAILS (per CLAUDE.md):

Vote Weight:
  • Each vote = user.total_area (in square meters)
  • Stored in: meeting_vote_records.vote_weight
  • Sourced from: users.total_area at vote time

Vote Uniqueness:
  • UNIQUE constraint: (meeting_id, agenda_item_id, voter_id)
  • Repeat voting: UPDATE existing record (never INSERT)
  • Prevents multiple votes per resident per agenda item

Objection Handling:
  • Table: meeting_agenda_comments
  • comment_type: 'comment' (normal) or 'objection' (against vote)
  • counter_proposal: Alternative proposal (optional when voting AGAINST)
  • Auto-triggered: When resident votes AGAINST agenda item

Quorum Calculation:
  • Requirement: 50%+ of total building area
  • Example: If building = 5000 m², need 2500 m² participation
  • Storage: meeting_building_settings.quorum_percent (default 50)

Protocol Generation:
  • Trigger: After close-voting
  • Source: meeting_agenda_comments.content (not separate comments table)
  • Includes: Aggregated votes, objections, counter-proposals
  • Signing: Via ContractQRCode.tsx (resident QR signature)

TABLES INVOLVED (10 tables):
  1. meetings              - Main meeting records
  2. meeting_schedule_options - Date options for voting
  3. meeting_schedule_votes   - Resident votes on dates
  4. meeting_agenda_items     - Agenda points + attachments (JSON)
  5. meeting_vote_records     - Votes WITH vote_weight field
  6. meeting_voting_units     - Voting unit definitions
  7. meeting_building_settings - Quorum configuration
  8. meeting_eligible_voters  - Who can vote
  9. meeting_participated_voters - Who did vote
  10. meeting_agenda_comments - Comments & objections
